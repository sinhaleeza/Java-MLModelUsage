Realization of SOA is a challenge and requires well designed common mechanisms to define, create, manage, services through standards compliant pre-built, pre-configured set of functionalities. In this paper we present a solution, experiences in developing an automated resource management system (ARMS), for workforce management, as part of the field services optimization programme of a large telecom services provider. We present a model for SOA realization and application of this model through Radien - a J2EE based SOA framework
